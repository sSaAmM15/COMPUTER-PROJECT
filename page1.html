Resident Evil 4 Remake

Get to know the famous characters of Resident Evil 4 Remake!

Character Description Image
Leon S. Kennedy Leon, a government agent, is the protagonist of Resident Evil 4. He is tasked with rescuing the President's daughter from a mysterious village in Spain.
Ashley Graham Ashley is the daughter of the U.S. President. She becomes the target of a cult and must be rescued by Leon, who protects her throughout the game.
Ada Wong Ada is a mysterious spy with a complicated relationship with Leon. She plays a key role in the plot, often helping Leon but keeping her motives hidden.
The Merchant He is a weapons peddler who dresses in a long, black coat. He carries a wide selection of weapons inside his huge backpack and coat.
